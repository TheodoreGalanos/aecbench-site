aec-bench is an open-source platform for benchmarking AI agents on real Architecture, Engineering and Construction (AEC) tasks.

## Why aec-bench?

Large language models are increasingly used in engineering workflows — from drafting calculations to reviewing designs. But how do we measure their actual capability on domain-specific tasks?

aec-bench provides:

- **500+ seed tasks** across five engineering disciplines (civil, electrical, ground, mechanical, structural)
- **Multiple agent harnesses** for different execution strategies (tool loop, RLM, lambda-RLM, direct)
- **Automated scoring** with domain-specific reward signals
- **Reproducible benchmarking** with versioned datasets and standardised evaluation

## Disciplines

aec-bench covers five core engineering disciplines:

| Discipline | Example tasks |
|------------|--------------|
| Civil | Road geometry, drainage design, hydraulic calculations, earthworks |
| Electrical | Cable sizing, fault current analysis, lighting design, power systems |
| Ground | Foundation design, slope stability, retaining wall analysis |
| Mechanical | HVAC sizing, fire protection, piping design, acoustic analysis |
| Structural | Steel/concrete member design, seismic analysis, connection design |
